About Me

Hello! I'm James!

I'm a recently graduated physics major from San Jose State University!

I'm very interested in computational physics.

I believe numerical simulation to be the most powerful tool for describing and predicting phenomenon in our universe.

There exists many phenomenon that the "computer of the universe" can run so effortlessly, but my laptop unfortunately cannot without tremendous difficulty.

This fact alone inspires me to push the limits of modern computation so we can have a better understanding of the world by being able to simulate our own.
